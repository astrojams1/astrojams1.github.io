Voice Commands Catalog

A catalog of useful voice commands for interacting with dictation services.

Commands

  • To backspace click your tongue
  • To print Ellipsis: say Dot, dot, dot
  • To print Emdash: say Emdash
  • To return to new line click your tongue twice
  • To print Period: say Period
